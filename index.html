Battle of the Ships

How to Play:
1. Each player has three boats to defend and three boats to attack. The
boats vary in length from 3 to 5 cells.
2. Each player attacks the opposite 10x10 grid by clicking on an empty cell.
3. A successful attack displays a boat (⛵️) emoji, while an unsuccessful
attack displays a red x (❌) emoji.
4. The boat tracker displays a filled-in black bar once a boat has been sunk.
5. The first player to sink all three opponent's ships wins!
